In the 1970s, a gangster named Rocky goes undercover as a slave to assassinate the owner of a notorious gold mine known as the Kolar Gold Fields.

MAX

Release Year: 2024

"Max" (2024) is a Kannada-language action thriller featuring Sudeep as a principled police officer entangled in a high-stakes political conspiracy. Critics have praised its gripping narrative and Sudeep's commanding performance, though some note a lack of memorable dialogues and a compelling antagonist.

KANTARA

Release Year: 2022

Kantara is a visually stunning masterpiece that blends folklore, action, and spirituality with gripping storytelling. Rishab Shetty's performance and the intense climax make it an unforgettable cinematic experience.

SALAAR

Release Year: 2022

"Salaar" delivers a gripping tale of power and brotherhood, with Prabhas excelling as an invincible hero in this action-packed thriller. However, some critics feel it falls short, offering a repetitive storyline that lacks freshness.
